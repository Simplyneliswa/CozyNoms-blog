From Call Center to Code: My Self-Taught Developer’s Journey.

At 27, I made a dramatic shiftfrom my routine call center job to dive into the world of web development. It wasn’t the expected career move, but it sparked a deep curiosity in me.

What started as a side interest quickly evolved into a full-blown passion for coding.

There are moments of frustration when the pieces don’t seem to fit, but then come those rewarding “Aha!” moments when everything clicks into place.

Each day brings a new challenge, a new skill to master, and a new problem to solve. I’m still learning, and every step of the way feels like an exciting adventure I eagerly embrace.

For me, coding is more than just writing lines of code; it’s about logical thinking, problem-solving

and developing the patience to keep going. It’s a journey of growth, both in skills and confidence.I’m proud of the progress I’ve made and thrilled about where this coding adventure will lead me next.
